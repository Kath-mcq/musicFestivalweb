About Us

Want to learn more the Foyle Music Festival? Look no further!

Foyle Music Festival

The Foyle Music Festival is a very beloved summer event. The perfect summer event to build memories. This event has been around for a few years now and has always been a smashing success with young people.

Artists

This year for the event, we wanted to focus more on alt/rock and more loud music. A wide range of artists will be preforming, such as Kneecap. Check out Line-up for more information!

Camping

Due to the success this event has had, the event has been extended into a week, so camping accommdations have been avaliable. Camping is optional, please contant us for more information.

Tickets & Age

As stated in our FAQ, 16 and those older may attend the event. Due to the amount of attraction the festival gets and the artists preforming this year, it wouldn't be a suitable place for kids. Tickets are avaliable for teenagers, adults and families. More information is avaliable in Tickets.
